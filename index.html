Crystals Collector!

Gameplay

The head miner has collected a certain amount of crystals each game round.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of crystals to your total collection.


You win the game by matching your total crystals to the head miner's crystal amount, you lose the game if your total crystals goes above the head miner's. Stop making him look bad, geez!


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time the game starts, the value of the crystals will change.
